The Iraqi Girl

Trilogy Book 1

Premise

The Iraqi Girl, volume one of The Iraqi Girl Trilogy, introduces readers to Massouma Qassim Ibrahim, who is sexually abused by U.S. soldiers at the age of five. This volume questions the morality of enhanced interrogation, highlights the disgusting practice of female genital mutilation in a graphic way, and sets the stage for revenge and the vigilante justice that are to follow in volumes 2 and 3 of The Iraqi Girl Trilogy.
